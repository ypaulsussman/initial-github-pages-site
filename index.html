About Me.

An expert communicator and passionate builder, I’ve spent the last twelve years developing the best language-learning experience for a given situation. Whether as an instructor, teacher-trainer, curriculum-designer, manager, and more, I’ve always always sought the perfect integration of pedagogy with technology. Having spent the first half of 2017 transitioning fully into the “technology" half of that equation, I’ve recently completed Prime Digital Academy’s full-stack software developer program. I’m now looking to work with a committed team on meaningful, human-centered web applications. Outside the professional sphere, I care about about helping and welcoming new Americans, the structure(s) of language(s), and human capital in all its forms.
